Stanford Cardinal Logo Colors

Stanford Cardinal Logo

The Stanford Cardinal logo has red and white colors and a stylized letter S object with a tree inside. The Stanford Cardinal logo meaning symbolizes the Stanford university initial as well as the El Palo Alto Redwood tree.
