Texas Longhorns Logo Colors

The Texas Longhorns logo colors are burnt orange, dark gray, and white. The burnt orange color code for the Texas Longhorns logo is Pantone: PMS 159 C, Hex Color: #BF5700, RGB: (191, 87, 0), CMYK: (0, 65, 100, 9). The dark gray color code for the Texas Longhorns logo is Pantone: PMS 432 C, Hex Color: #333F48, RGB: (51, 63, 72), CMYK: (65, 43, 26, 78). The white color code for the Texas Longhorns logo is Hex Color: #FFFFFF, RGB: (255, 255, 255), CMYK: (0, 0, 0, 0).

Texas Longhorns Logo

The Texas Longhorns logo has burnt orange, dark gray, and white colors and features a minimalistic depiction of a bulls head with long horns that extend to its sides. The Texas Longhorns logo meaning symbolizes the breed of cattle where the team got its nickname from, the Texas longhorn.
